Courses We Offer

Generally, we sew clothes, fabrics and apparel for a living.
We work at altering clothing and apparel to fit a client/customer. We can make clothes as well and might be tasked with hemming and mending.
Usually, we work more with suits and coats and even fancy dresses such as ball gowns.
